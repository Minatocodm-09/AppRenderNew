💻 What is Technology Knowledge?

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes. It is the engine of modern civilization, driving change in every sector of human life, from communication and healthcare to space exploration and environmental management. Technology knowledge is not just for software engineers; it is an essential literacy for anyone navigating the 21st century.

Technology is broadly categorized into Hardware (physical components), Software (programs and instructions), and Emerging Trends (AI, Blockchain, Quantum Computing). It also includes the study of cybersecurity, networking, and the ethical implications of digital innovation.

Why Technology Knowledge is Important: Digital literacy is the key to personal and professional growth. Understanding technology empowers individuals to use digital tools safely and effectively. In competitive exams and job interviews, technological awareness is a core requirement, testing your grasp of modern innovation. Beyond work, it allows you to participate in the global digital economy and stay connected in an increasingly automated world.
